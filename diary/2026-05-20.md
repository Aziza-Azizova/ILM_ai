## Problems encountered

`pdf-parse` caused the most pain. After fixing file upload validation (NestJS `FileTypeValidator` can't validate files saved to disk — it needs the buffer in memory), PDF processing still failed with `pdfParse is not a function`. The installed version had changed its export shape. Tried three different import approaches — all failed for different reasons. Also hit MUI v9 breaking changes that weren't obvious from the docs.

## Solutions (if any)

Pinned `pdf-parse@1.1.1` which exports the function directly. For MUI v9 replaced all `<Grid item xs md>` with `<Grid size={{}} >` and moved typography props into `sx={{}}`.
